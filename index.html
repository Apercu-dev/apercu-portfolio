APERÇU

Hello, I'm Aperçu — graphic designer, art director and artist based in Nantes, France.

Branding, creative direction, spatial design, video, visual identity, painting, jewellery, fashion — everything I do feeds into a single body of work. Since 2017, I've been building a practice that refuses to stay in one lane, driven by a single obsession : finding the right form for every intention, and pushing it as far as it can go.
