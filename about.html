— 01 / Who I am

A designer who
refuses to stay
in one lane.

I'm Mael Ballay — plurimedia designer with a DNMADE Espace diploma, building a creative practice across disciplines. Graphic design, art direction, spatial design, painting, jewellery, clothing, video — I don't choose. I use all of it.

Design, to me, is more than visual. It's a way to create emotions, tell stories, and challenge conventions — whether through a brand identity, an immersive retail concept, or a handcrafted piece made from recycled materials.

My inspiration comes from urban landscapes, raw materials, contemporary art, and the imperfections found in nature. Industrial meets organic. Structure meets expression. That tension is where my work lives.
